### 6.2 Voting Rights

| Matter | Required Approval |
|---|---|
| Ordinary business | Majority of board |
| New equity issuance | Board + majority of preferred |
| Sale / acquisition | Board + 60% of preferred + majority of common |
| Budget >10% variance | Board approval |
| Executive hiring/firing | Board approval |
| Debt >$500K | Board + majority of preferred |

### 6.3 Investor Information Rights

| Deliverable | Frequency | Recipients |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly financials + KPIs | Monthly | All preferred shareholders |
| Board package | Quarterly | Board members |
| Annual audited financials | Annually | All preferred shareholders |
| Cap table updates | After each grant/exercise | All preferred shareholders |
| Budget & plan | Annually | Board approval required |

## 7. Secondary Sale Provisions

- Company has Right of First Refusal (ROFR) on all secondary sales
- Board approval required for secondary transactions
- No secondary sales permitted until $25M ARR milestone (waivable by board)
- Co-sale rights for investors on founder secondary sales
- Lock-up: 180 days post-IPO for all shareholders
