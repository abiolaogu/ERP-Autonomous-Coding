## 1. GTM Philosophy

Developer tools sell bottom-up. Individual developers try the tool, love it, tell their team, and the team tells the organization. Our GTM leverages this dynamic: a 30-day free trial for 10 developers seeds viral adoption within engineering teams. By the time we engage the VP Engineering for an enterprise contract, 50+ developers are already using the platform.

## 6. Expansion Playbook

**Land:** 10-50 developers in one team (backend, platform, or security).
**Expand Phase 1:** Adjacent teams request access after seeing completion rates and review speed.
**Expand Phase 2:** VP Engineering standardizes across all teams after seeing aggregate metrics.
**Expand Phase 3:** Enterprise tier upgrade for SSO, architecture enforcement, and on-prem.

Average expansion: 3.2x developer count within 12 months. Top customer grew from 40 to 400 developers in 10 months.

## 7. Pricing Strategy
